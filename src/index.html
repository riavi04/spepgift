Build your own

Each row is a bird or a drum. Click the squares to place hits. The number next to each bird shifts its pitch in semitones, which also makes it shorter and brighter as it goes up. Write up to eight bars, then pick how long the finished track should run.

The aviary

Every sound in here is a real wild recording of a real bird, made by a person who went outside with a microphone. Their names are on the cards. Click a clip to hear it.

All recordings come from xeno-canto, a library of wild bird sound shared by recordists around the world, under Creative Commons licenses. Each clip credits its recordist and links back to the original. Clips have been trimmed, filtered and level matched for use here. Photographs come from Wikimedia Commons and are credited on each card. Set in EB Garamond by Georg Duffner and Octavio Pardo, under the SIL Open Font License.
